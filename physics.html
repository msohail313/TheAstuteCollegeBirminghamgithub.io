Physics

Physics is a branch of Science it is defined as the study of physical properties, process or natural phenomena of matter, particular system or composition of something. The subject majors on heat, motion, momentum, fluids, circuits, light, radiation, electrify magnetism, mechanics, the structure of atoms and other related areas. It behavioural study is very vast and expanding day by day, but we need to start from baby step and improve our knowledge step by step.
